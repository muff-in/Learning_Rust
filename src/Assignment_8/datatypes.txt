* Primitive Types
- Rust has a couple of types that are considered primitive. 
- That means they are built-in to the language. There are different data types used for different purposes.

* Scalar Type
- They store a single value.

Below is the list of scalar types:

> Integers
> Floats
> Boolean
> Character

* Compound Type
- They group multiple values in one variable. 

Below is the list of compound types:

> Array
> Tuple
